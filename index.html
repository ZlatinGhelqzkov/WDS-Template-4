Apollonia Festival of arts - sozopol

07/08/2015

read full article

The Apollonia Festival of Arts will take place on the 28th of August to the 5 of September in the seaside town of Sozopol. Sozopol is a wonderful place for such a cultural celebration, due to its picturesque Old Town neighborhood and numeros historical landmarks. The 9 day festival will present a plethora of musical and theatrical acts, as well as various art galleries exhibitions, dancers, architects and writers. There are 39 different events on the program, featuring renowned Bulgarian and international performers and artists.
